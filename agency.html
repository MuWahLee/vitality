Core Services

Here is an overview of what we do.

Social Media Strategy and Planning

SOCIAL MEDIA STRATEGY AND PLANNING

In the world of citizen journalists and bloggers, everyone’s a reporter, everyone’s an influencer. And companies or governments that don’t recognize the power of these “new influencers” in a crisis have suffered as a result.

How do you identify influencers and determine how influential are they?

How do you prioritize time and resources in engaging a whole new world of influencers?

Social Media Management

SOCIAL MEDIA MANAGEMENT

Your brand now has more than one website to manage – it has a hundred or even more “web properties” built to ensure that you are “socially omnipresent”.

What happens when these “web assets” are poorly managed? They turn into “web liabilities” that consume countless hours and marketing dollars

How do you create systems that ensure every single web property works together to create value and greater returns on the investment made in creating content and engaging stakeholders (customers, media, bloggers etc.) through them?

Social Measurement

SOCIAL MEASUREMENT AND ANALYTICS

With the explosion of social media platforms and new metrics, measurement is far more complex than before. It's important to have well-designed, measurement systems that have profound impact on guiding the efforts of your social media engagement teams. It's also important to have the right measurement tools and methodologies in place so it doesn't become an onerous task that becomes counterproductive. There is no perfect system, or tool, because your needs, resources, and values are different.

We design measurement strategies that are aligned to your business needs, and that empower you to effectively measure campaign results.

Social Content

SOCIAL CONTENT DEVELOPMENT

Your stakeholders are drowning in information. The volume of online content and number of new social media platforms/channels are exploding.

How do you create content that grabs attention, gains trust and is spread via word-of-mouth?

What are the ingredients to “viral” and how do we create compelling content that has desired impact for brands online. (Infographics, Data Visualisation, “YouTube Videos”, and more.)

CEO/Senior Exec Branding

CEO / SENIOR EXECUTIVE BRANDING

We operate in a personality-driven business environment, and the personal brand, credibility and influence of a senior leader (CEOs, Ministers, and other leaders etc.) have deep and profound impact on a company/organization.

From what shows up on search engines when individuals are searched-for, to the quality and visibility of their thinking on new, social media channels – these factors play a crucial role in shaping the perceived value and credibility of a company. Take Dominic Orr, CEO of Aruba Networks for example – when his name is searched for, alongside the company name “Aruba”, you’ll find at the top, an article dated 30 Oct 2007, with the headline “How Dominic Orr almost ruined his life. Being 'ruthlessly aggressive' took Dominic Orr to the top of the corporate world.”

Internal Communications

INTERNAL COMMUNICATIONS AND COLLABORATION SYSTEMS

Business now takes place at the speed of light, and stakeholders increasingly expect “Instant Communication”. Customers expect you to respond immediately to complaints they Tweet publicly, or post on your brand’s Facebook wall. Employees expect leaders to reach them before the office gossip.

How do you re-engineer internal systems and processes to respond as quickly to new opportunities and challenges?

How do you exploit new web technologies that will accelerate the speed and quality of internal communication and collaboration?

Intergrated Newsroom

INTEGRATED NEWS ROOM MANAGEMENT

The News Room is evolving at a rapid pace to remain competitive in today’s media world. The responsibilities and workflow of reporters too, have changed.

How do we engage “old media” that are evolving in their approach to news reporting?

How do we set up a news room that’s perfectly aligned with the needs of media and analysts?

Online Rapid Response and Crisis Management

ONLINE RAPID RESPONSE AND CRISIS MANAGEMENT

Because conversations explode every time there is a crisis, and because today, most issues first break out on Social Media channels, it’s important to have a system in place that’s just as dynamic, nimble and responsive in the “new media environment”.

Mobile Innovation

MOBILE INNOVATION

Mobile is the new normal, but how do you stand out among all the noise?

New technology that grabs your audience's attention while staying out of the way is essential.

Augmented reality, gamification, and location-awareness, are just some of the ways that we help you to tell your compelling story on mobile platforms.

Web Creation

WEB CREATION

In a multi-device world, your content must be dynamically tailored for wherever and whenever it is consumed.

We create device-independent (Responsive Design) web sites and landing pages that ensures your audience heeds your Call to Action.

SEO/SEM

SEO/SEM

Great content is useless if it cannot be found, or is drowned out by your competitors’ voices. Establishing your ranking on search engines such as Google, Yahoo! and Baidu takes long-term planning and short-term execution excellence, supported by an intimate and up-to-date knowledge of each platform’s latest algorithm changes.
