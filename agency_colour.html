About Us

Our Story


The last thing the world needs is another digital agency. We know that. Not that they aren’t important, but because some days it’s hard to tell one agency from another. Digital marketing has become a buzzword, gradually losing meaning as advertising, social media and PR agencies all redefine it from their own frames of reference.

When we founded The Daylight Partnership, we decided that we would move away from religious wars. Let’s focus on solving problems. Perception problems affecting sales. Communication problems losing the company partners. A story told badly leading to misunderstanding by government officials. An unexpected crisis eroding consumer confidence in the brand. In other words, business problems.


In our view, Digital Marketing solves problems with tools of the trade from social media, PR, design, communication, mobile, and web.


One day, it won’t be Digital Marketing anymore, it will just be Marketing.

SOCIAL MEDIA STRATEGY AND PLANNING

How do you identify influencers and determine how influential they are?

How do you prioritize time and resources in engaging a whole new world of influencers?

Let us tell you more.


Social Media Management
SOCIAL MEDIA MANAGEMENT

Your brand now has more than one website to manage – it has a hundred or even more “web properties” built to ensure that you are “socially omnipresent”.

What happens when these “web assets” are poorly managed? They turn into “web liabilities” that consume countless hours and marketing dollars.

How do you create systems that ensure every single web property works together to create value and greater returns on the investment made in creating content and engaging stakeholders (customers, media, bloggers etc.) through them?

Let us tell you more.


Social Measurement
SOCIAL MEASUREMENT AND ANALYTICS

With the explosion of social media platforms and new metrics, measurement is far more complex than before. It's important to have well-designed measurement systems that have a profound impact on guiding the efforts of your social media engagement teams. It's also important to have the right measurement tools and methodologies in place so it doesn't become an onerous task that becomes counterproductive. There is no perfect system, or tool, because your needs, resources, and values are different.

We design measurement strategies that are aligned to your business needs, and that empowers you to effectively measure campaign results.

CEO/Senior Exec Branding
CEO / SENIOR EXECUTIVE BRANDING

We operate in a personality-driven business environment, and the personal brand, credibility and influence of senior leaders (CEOs, Ministers, and other leaders etc.) have a deep and profound impact on a company/organization.

From what shows up on search engines when individuals are searched for, to the quality and visibility of their thinking on new, social media channels – these factors play a crucial role in shaping the perceived value and credibility of a company. Take Dominic Orr, CEO of Aruba Networks for example – when his name is searched for, alongside the company name “Aruba”, you’ll find at the top, an article dated 30 Oct 2007, with the headline “How Dominic Orr almost ruined his life. Being 'ruthlessly aggressive' took Dominic Orr to the top of the corporate world.”

Let us tell you more.
